Tear of the Forest

Tear of the Forest is a 2D interactive story with an isometric view. Tear wakes up wondering what happened to her. She does not remember anything, it seems she has lost all her memories. She discovers that she can possess certain animals. Help Tear uncover parts of her past to help her get home.

When Spring Came

When Spring Came places the player in the shoes of a person who is struggling with depression. The player will control Kana in her daily life while getting getting a peek at what it may feel like when a person is struggling with depression.

Goblin Stone

Goblin Stone is a turn-based RPG told from a goblin's perspective. Adventurers have pushed goblins and orcs to near extinction: it's now time to fight back. Gather a ragtag group of goblins and journey through an epic procedurally generated world, as you change their fate in this narrated adventure.
